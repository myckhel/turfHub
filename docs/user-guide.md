# TurfMate User Guide

## Introduction
TurfMate is a digital platform designed to streamline mini football match sessions for both players and turf managers. This guide covers the main features, user flows, and how to get started.

## 1. Getting Started
- **Sign Up:** Register with your name, email, and password.
- **Login:** Access your dashboard and join or create turfs.
- **Email Verification:** Secure your account via email confirmation.

---

## 2. Player Experience
- **Join a Turf:** Browse active/scheduled match sessions.
- **Pay & Secure Slot:** Pay online to join a team slot.
- **Play & Track:** Play matches; system tracks team status and match history.
- **Notifications:** Get notified when your team plays next.

---

## 3. Turf Manager Experience
- **Create Sessions:** Start new match sessions and define team slots.
- **Assign Players:** Auto/manual assignment of players to teams.
- **Manage Events:** Track scores, substitutions, cards, and match events.
- **Queue Logic:** System auto-queues next match based on results.

---

## 4. Match Sessions & Team Rotation
- **Win:** Team stays, next team steps in.
- **Loss:** Team moves to end of queue.
- **Draw:** Both teams step out, random team re-enters first next round.
- **Session Archive:** Managers can end sessions and archive records.

---

## 5. Payment & Security
- **Online Payments:** Secure payment for team slots.
- **Authentication:** Token-based API security (see API documentation).
- **Role Management:** Admin, player, and manager roles for turfs.

---

## 6. Mobile Features
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for all devices.
- **Touch-Friendly:** Easy navigation and large buttons.
- **Fast Loading:** Performance optimized for mobile.

---

## 7. FAQ
- **How do I join a match?**
  - Register, verify email, and pay for a slot in an active session.
- **How are teams assigned?**
  - Teams are auto-assigned or managed by turf managers.
- **What happens after my team loses?**
  - Your team moves to the end of the queue; next team plays.
- **Is my payment secure?**
  - Yes, all payments are processed securely.
